About NutriCrate

Fuel Up, Feel Great

Our Mission

At NutriCrate, we believe that discovering your next favorite supplement should be as exciting as opening a gift. Our mission is to help fitness enthusiasts, athletes, and everyday movers explore a wide variety of trusted nutrition brands—without the commitment or guesswork. Each month, we deliver a curated mystery box of energy drinks, protein powders, bars, and performance snacks designed to keep you fueled, motivated, and feeling great.

How We Started

NutriCrate began in 2024 when a small group of college fitness lovers realized how hard it was to find new supplements without buying huge tubs or full cases. What started as a dorm-room idea—sharing sample packs and rating them for fun—grew into a passion project to connect people with products that actually work for their lifestyle. We launched NutriCrate to make discovery easy, affordable, and fun again.

Where We’re Headed

As we grow, our goal is to partner with more brands, expand our selection to include hydration, vitamins, and recovery products, and build a personalized recommendation engine so each box feels made just for you. We’re also working on integrating sustainable packaging and carbon-neutral shipping to make every delivery a little better for the planet.

Whether you’re chasing PRs at the gym or just trying to stay energized through the day, NutriCrate is here to help you fuel up and feel great.
